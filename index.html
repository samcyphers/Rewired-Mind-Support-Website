Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is The Cortina Method?+
The Cortina Method (TCM) is a brain-based healing methodology that uses neuroscience-backed protocols to resolve physical pain, trauma, anxiety, depression, grief, substance use, and other behavioral health concerns. Unlike traditional talk therapy, TCM does not require you to relive painful experiences — it works with your brain's natural processes to create lasting change.
What age groups do you work with?+
We work with people of all ages: young children, youth, teens, and adults. Our approach can be tailored to any developmental stage without compromising treatment efficacy because our approach is based on how all brains work on a neurological level, not cognitive development level.
What happens in a session?+
Unlike traditional talk therapy, the practitioner does most of the talking during the session to guide you through the TCM protocol. The client will NOT have to relive trauma or share in depth about what they are coming to the session to resolve. Together we use body movement, cognitive restructuring, divergent experience and reconsolidation techniques to rewire the neural networks in your brain to find resolution to your problem. Sessions are calm, conversational, and focused on your healing. You will leave feeling lighter, not heavier.
Can sessions be conducted virtually?+
Yes, we are happy to conduct all sessions virtually and as a result can provide TCM services anywhere in the world! Effectiveness ratings for this treatment approach are equal whether sessions are virtual or in person.
Do I have to relive my trauma?+
No. The emphasis is entirely on people NOT having to relive or re-experience distressing emotions. You do not have to re-experience the pain you've already endured and survived. TCM works through brain optimization and reprocessing — not re-traumatization.
How many sessions will I need?+
TCM is designed for efficiency. The initial 2-hour session provides profound transformation for 90% of guests. 8% of guests need at least one additional visit, and 2% of guests require ongoing support or find the treatment ineffective. We can discuss this more at your discovery call and after your initial visit to determine the right level of support for you.
What does the 90% effectiveness rate mean?+
TCM has a documented 90% effectiveness rate after one visit across its clinical applications. Because root causes are pinpointed and resolved — and new neural pathways are created — the changes are enduring, not just temporary relief. An additional 8% of guests require at least one support visit to achieve full resolution or to work through problems not addressed in the first visit. The remaining 2% of guests do not find total resolution. This may be due to factors such as active substance use altering their brain chemistry, or feeling unready or unwilling to let go of their trauma, grief, or other concerns.

Currently, all Rewired Mind services are private pay. If you want treatment but are worried about the cost, please bring this up in your discovery call and we can discuss possible payment plans or financial assistance options you may qualify for.
